Unit 3

In this unit I researched three types of methods. One:Exploratory research, intends merely to explore the research questions and does not intend to offer final and conclusive solutions to existing problems. Two:Conclusive research, this design provides a way to verify and quantify the insights gained from exploratory research. The last type is:Qualitative research involves looking in-depth at non-numerical data. It is a research method used extensively by researchers studying opinions, themes and motivations as well as artifacts.

Unit 4

In this unit I learnd that research methods are different from research methodologies because they are the ways in which you will collect the data for your research project. Things like closed-ended questionnaires/survey and Structured interviews. The method that I found to be most helpful to me is the interview process. I learned the following during the process: Keep the questions brief and simple. Eliminate any potential bias from your questions. If your topic is very sensitive, you may want to ask indirect questions rather than direct ones. If you are using a closed-ended question, try to offer every possible answer that a participant could give to that question. Do not ask questions that assume something of the participant. Try and keep the questionnaire as short as possible.
