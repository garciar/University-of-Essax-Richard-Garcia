Unit 5

In this module I reviewd what make up a network.

I learned about components such as switches, routers, firewalls and gateways and the role such equipment plays in different computing environments I learned that the two most important components of a network are thee switch and router. Switches allow devices on your network to communicate with each other, as well as with other networks, creating a network of shared resources. Routers connect multiple networks together. They also connect computers on those networks to the Internet. Routers enable all networked computers to share a single Internet connection.
